# Safety and Privacy

PocketTrainer is general fitness guidance for adults. It does not diagnose pain, injury, mobility conditions, or disease.

## Movement safety

- A lesson scores only when required landmarks, camera orientation, and confidence gates pass.
- Skipping a required movement phase never counts as a valid repetition.
- A pain report stops the exercise, blocks progression, and recommends a safer alternative.
- Harder movement variants require both account level and repeated demonstrated mastery.
- A qualified fitness professional reviews every published definition and known limitation.

## Camera privacy

- Camera frames are processed on-device and not stored by default.
- Pose landmarks exist in device memory for the active session only.
- The backend stores derived rep and session summaries, never raw landmark sequences.
- Optional replay and model-training contribution are outside V1 and would require separate consent.

## Data controls

Consent categories remain independent. The product must support data export, account deletion, token revocation, and deletion of imported or optional content before public beta.
