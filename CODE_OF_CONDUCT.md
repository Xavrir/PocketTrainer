# Code of Conduct

PocketTrainer contributors must make participation respectful, harassment-free, and welcoming regardless of background, identity, experience, body type, fitness level, or ability.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, sharing private information, and body-shaming. Exercise feedback and community discussions must focus on movement and safety—not appearance or weight.

Repository maintainers may remove content or participation that violates these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner.
